Research focuses on:

Mr. Jaya Prakash has focused on the following research area; The main theme of my PhD work is on Biomass estimation and Soil carbon estimation of selected Indian tropical forests sites, where I am intended to assimilate various remotely sensed observations like Rainfall, Evapotranspiration, Soil moisture and other biophysical parameters.


  1. Remote Sensing and GIS application in Forestry
  2. Advanced SAR techniques for forest applications
  3. Microwave remote sensing and its application
Contribution to Project's
  • Species Level Mapping and AG Biomass Estimation from Airborne QuadPol SAR Data with other data sets using two Machine Learning Techniques from Related Forest Sites in India. (Sponsored: Space Applications Centre, Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), Ahmedabad)
