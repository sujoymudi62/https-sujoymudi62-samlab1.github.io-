About

Ms. Roma's research is broadly interdisciplinary and studies the effect of Coastal Ocean-Atmosphere dynamics at the Terrestrial Vegetation Ecosystems. She is a Civil Engineering graduate from the Cochin University of Science and Technology and obtained MTech degree in Ocean Technology. Her research is mainly surrounding on the studies related to carbon sequestration via ocean and terrestrial vegetation. Currently focus is given to the modeling of Terrestrial Primary Productivity across India using a set of diagnostic & prognostic Ecosystem Models, and various climatic factors influencing the present and future functionality of Ecosystem.
